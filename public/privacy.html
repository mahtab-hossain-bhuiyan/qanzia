Privacy Policy

How we protect your data

At Qnaxia, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Basic usage data (pages visited, time spent on site)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Cookies for session management and preferences
  • Any information you voluntarily provide (email for newsletters)

How We Use Your Information

  • To improve our content and user experience
  • To understand how visitors use our site
  • To respond to your inquiries
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help remember your preferences and analyze site traffic.

Google AdSense and Third-Party Cookies

Qnaxia displays Google AdSense ads served by Google. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites. The DoubleClick DART cookie enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites. You can opt out of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad Settings page or by opting out of third-party vendor use of cookies through the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

Third-Party Vendors

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These vendors may collect information about your visits to our site and other websites to provide relevant advertisements. These third-party vendors include:

  • Google (AdSense, Analytics) — For ad delivery and traffic analysis
  • Our hosting provider — For site functionality

Each third-party vendor has its own privacy policy governing their data collection practices.

Your Choices

  • Browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Google Ad Settings to personalize ads
  • Opt out of third-party cookies via NAI
  • Clear your browser cookies at any time

GDPR Rights (EEA Users)

If you're located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of sale of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and update the effective date.
